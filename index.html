How to Survive
  1. Explore the forest to gather wood with the axe and stone with the pickaxe.
  2. Use the hoe to till soil, plant seeds with the seeder, then harvest ripe crops with your hands for food.
  3. Assign roles to servants in the side panel: lumberjacks chop wood, miners quarry stone, and farmers tend crops.
  4. Open the build menu (B) to place huts, fields, and roads when you have enough resources.
  5. Fields feed your workers, silos expand food storage, wells provide water rations, and roads boost travel speed.
  6. Keep an eye on the day/night cycle at the top of the screen—travel home before darkness slows your progress.
  7. Save often and revisit cleared tiles; trees and rocks will grow back after a few days.
